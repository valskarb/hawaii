Hawaii

The Destination Waiting to Be Scratched Off My Map

My Scratch-Off Map Story

For my birthday, a family member gave me a scratch-off world map. Every time I visit a new place, I scratch it off and reveal the color underneath. Over the years, I’ve scratched off many states — but Hawaii remains untouched. It stands out as one of the last places I have yet to explore.

Anticipation & Future Memories

I don’t have memories in Hawaii yet — and that’s exactly what makes it special. Instead of nostalgia, I feel anticipation. I imagine walking on warm beaches, watching sunsets over the ocean, and experiencing the culture firsthand. Hawaii represents future memories waiting to happen.

Why Hawaii is So Special

  • Stunning beaches and volcanic landscapes
  • Unique Hawaiian culture and traditions
  • Amazing tropical food and fresh seafood
  • Beautiful weather almost year-round

The Dream

One day, I will finally visit Hawaii. When I do, I’ll come home, scratch it off my map, and remember the journey that turned anticipation into real memories.
